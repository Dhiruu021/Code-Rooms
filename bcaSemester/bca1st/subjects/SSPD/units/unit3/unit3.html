Persuasive Presentation

Key Elements of Persuasive Presentations:

  • Interactive Argument: Engaging with the audience during the presentation.
  • Attracting External Audience: Capturing the attention of the listeners.
  • Enacting Change: Changing the strategy based on audience feedback.

Self-Assessment

The Importance of Self-Assessment:

  • Self-Reflection: Understanding one's strengths and weaknesses.
  • Continuous Improvement: Working towards personal growth.
