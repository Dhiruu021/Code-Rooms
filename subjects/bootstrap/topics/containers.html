Bootstrap Containers

Overview of Bootstrap Containers

Bootstrap offers several types of containers to create responsive layouts. Containers help in managing the padding and centering of your content. Here are the different types of containers available in Bootstrap:

Standard Container


<div class="container">
    <h1>BOOTSTRAP</h1>
    <p>Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet consectetur adipisicing elit. Ea quam explicabo ullam perspiciatis. Illum quasi vitae debitis maxime, aperiam omnis delectus culpa corrupti deserunt fugiat veniam iure dolor fugit et.</p>
</div>

</div>

Fluid Container


<div class="container-fluid">
    <h1>BOOTSTRAP</h1>
    <p>Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet consectetur adipisicing elit. Ea quam explicabo ullam perspiciatis. Illum quasi vitae debitis maxime, aperiam omnis delectus culpa corrupti deserunt fugiat veniam iure dolor fugit et.</p>
    <br>

Conclusion

Choosing the right container type is essential for ensuring that your layout is responsive and visually appealing. Remember that Bootstrap alters the default font properties, which may require additional customization to match your design needs.
