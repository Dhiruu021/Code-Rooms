Strategic Thinking

Introduction to Strategic Thinking:

  • Being Proactive: Taking initiative and anticipating challenges.
  • Understanding Counter Arguments: Evaluating opposing views to strengthen one's position.
  • Optimizing with Experiences: Learning from past situations to improve future strategies.

Listening

Effective Listening Techniques:

  • Eye Contact: Helps in staying engaged and showing interest.
  • Being Attentive: Focused attention to ensure understanding.
  • Nonverbal Cues: Reading body language to understand the speaker’s emotions.

Active vs. Passive Listening

Comparing Active and Passive Listening:

  • Active Listening: Fully engaged, asking questions, and providing feedback.
  • Passive Listening: Just hearing the words without offering engagement.
