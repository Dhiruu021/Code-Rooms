Conflict Management

Strategies for Managing Conflict:

  • Active Listening: Understanding the other party's viewpoint.
  • Compromise: Finding a middle ground that satisfies both parties.
  • Staying Calm: Managing emotions during heated discussions.

Negotiation Skills

Effective Negotiation Techniques:

  • Preparation: Researching the other party's needs and priorities.
  • Building Rapport: Establishing trust and a positive relationship.
  • Finding Win-Win Solutions: Creating outcomes that benefit both sides.

Types of Negotiations

Different Types of Negotiations:

  • Distributive Negotiation: Competing over limited resources.
  • Integrative Negotiation: Seeking mutually beneficial solutions.
