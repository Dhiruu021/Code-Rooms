Understanding functional dependencies and their role in maintaining consistency and correctness in relational databases.

Learn about log-based recovery techniques and the importance of checkpoints in maintaining database consistency.

Deadlock Handling

Explore deadlock handling techniques in transaction processing systems to ensure smooth execution of concurrent transactions.
